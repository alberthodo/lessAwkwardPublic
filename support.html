Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock premium questions?

Tap the "Unlock Pro" button in the app to view subscription options. You can choose from weekly, monthly, or lifetime access. All purchases are managed through Apple's App Store.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you've previously purchased premium access, tap "Restore Purchases" in the paywall screen. This will restore your subscription or lifetime purchase on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription directly through your Apple ID settings:

  • Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
  • Find "Less Awkward" in your active subscriptions
  • You can cancel, change plans, or view billing history

Are my favorites saved?

Yes! Your favorite questions are saved locally on your device. If you sign in with Apple, your favorites may sync across devices using iCloud.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Once you've loaded questions in the app, you can use it offline. Premium questions require an active subscription, but once unlocked, you can access them offline too.

How do I delete my account?

If you signed in with Apple, you can revoke access in Settings → [Your Name] → Sign-In & Security → Apps Using Apple ID. To request deletion of any stored data, email us at support@lessawkward.app.

I'm having trouble with a purchase

Purchase issues are typically handled by Apple. Check your App Store purchase history, or contact Apple Support. If the issue persists, email us at support@lessawkward.app with your Apple ID email and we'll help investigate.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Email us at support@lessawkward.app with details about the bug or your feature suggestion.
